We have a block of rooms available at Hotel Deca (7 mins walk from the Department of Astronomy). These rooms are available the rate of $129/night (city-level, king bed) or $159/night (sky-level, king bed); parking is complimentary. The room block will be held until May 23, after this date reservations will still be accepted at the group rate based on hotel availability.

Reservations can be made by each individual by either calling the hotel directly at 1-800-899-0251 between the hours (PDT) of 8am-7pm, Monday through Friday and 10am-4pm on Saturday and Sunday or emailing requests to reservations@hoteldeca.com. To get the group rate, you will need to mention the AstroViz conference.

Please check with Hotel Deca when you make your reservation regarding their cancellation policies.
