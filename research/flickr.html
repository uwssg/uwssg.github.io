Gadget Examples: Flickr & Google Sky


This canvas consists of two independent gadgets: a viewport gadget and an image carousel gadget. The viewport gadget can be navigated by click-dragging to rotate the view, and scrolling to zoom in. The image carousel dynamically loads images from the astrometry.net flickr group pool. Mousing over an image will display it in a larger view, and clicking on an image will create an overlay in the viewport and print the image information to the screen. Finally, the slider can be used to adjust the transparency of the overlayed image in the viewport!
