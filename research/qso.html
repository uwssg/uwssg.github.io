Gadget Examples: Query & Visualizations


Filter for specific targets of your choice! Type your SQL query into the 'Query String' box. The SQL query example below returns u-g, g-r, r-i, i-z data from SDSS within the RA and DEC bounds specified:

SELECT TOP 500 objid,specObjId,ra,dec,modelMag_g,(modelMag_u - modelMag_g) AS u_g,(modelMag_g - modelMag_r) AS g_r,(modelMag_r - modelMag_i) AS r_i,(modelMag_i - modelMag_z) AS i_z FROM PhotoPrimary WHERE ra BETWEEN 166.5929564485714 AND 166.73451585376313 AND dec BETWEEN 24.92361956464075 AND 25.076380435359255 AND modelMag_g > -9999 ORDER BY modelMag_g ASC

Using the 3 plotters, select the data you want to visualize from the dataset returned from your query. You could choose to plot u_g vs g_r, g_r vs. r_i, and r_i vs i_z to see how your objects look in various color-color spaces.
